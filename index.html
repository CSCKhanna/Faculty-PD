Button Guide

Filters

Search narrows by title, provider, topic, description, access note, or source.

Sort orders by date, provider, or priority.

Timing limits the list to all year, upcoming, summer, fall, or spring.

Verified/free only hides items that need local access or cost confirmation.

Views and Exports

Cards is the full browsing view and moves the card results to the top of the viewport.

Timeline groups items by month and moves the timeline results to the top of the viewport.

Table is a compact list for scanning and moves the table results to the top of the viewport.

CSV, Calendar, Announcement, and Print export the current filtered list.

List Categories

Recommended is the main curated list. Click again to remove it from the current filter.

Advertise First shows the highest-confidence items to promote soon and can be combined with other list categories.

Newly Detected shows items found by the updater.

Hold List keeps useful items that need sponsorship or cost/access screening.

All clears the List filters while keeping any Provider, Topic, Search, Timing, or Verified/free filters.

Provider and Topic Chips

Provider chips show only trainings from selected organizations.

Topic chips show only selected themes such as AI, Writing, Promotion & Tenure, Teaching, or Mentoring.

Source links open the provider or evidence page for a card or access note.
